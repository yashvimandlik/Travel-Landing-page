Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Andaman 6 Nights 7 Days

Hey there, traveler! Ever dreamt of a place where the beaches are so dreamy they make you pinch yourself? Welcome to the Andaman & Nicobar Islands. Imagine a spot where the ocean kisses the shores like old friends and the history isn't just in books but standing tall in places like the Cellular Jail. It's a cocktail of sun-soaked sands, stories from the past, and adventures that'll make your heart race. Dive into clear waters, make friends with colorful fish, or just laze on the beach making sandcastles. Whether you're a thrill-seeker, a history buff, or just someone needing a break, these islands have a little magic for everyone. So, ready to let the Andamans charm you?The Andaman Islands are an Indian archipelago in the Bay of Bengal. These roughly 300 islands are known for their palm-lined, white-sand beaches, mangroves and tropical rainforests. Coral reefs supporting marine life such as sharks and rays make for popular diving and snorkeling sites. Indigenous Andaman Islanders inhabit the more remote islands, many of which are off limits to visitors.

By Air

Veer Savarkar International Airport in Port Blair is the gateway to the Andaman Islands. It is well connected with major Indian cities like Delhi, Chennai, and Mumbai.

By Sea

You can opt for a sea voyage from Chennai, Kolkata, or Visakhapatnam to Port Blair. However, it's a lengthier journey, taking about 60-70 hours.

Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair

Welcome to the captivating Andaman Islands! As you touch down at Veer Savarkar International Airport in Port Blair, your island journey begins. Get ready for a seamless transfer to your hotel, where you'll complete the check-in process. The adventure kicks off with a visit to Corbyn's Cove Beach—a true paradise where you can unwind, bask in the sun, and relish the mesmerizing evening ambiance. Don't miss out on the historical significance of the Cellular Jail and be prepared to be entranced by the mesmerizing Light and Sound show. Your night's retreat awaits in Port Blair.

Day 2: Ross Island and North Bay Island

Rise and shine after a delightful breakfast! Today, let's embark on a thrilling exploration. Hop aboard a ferry to Ross Island, a once-mighty British administrative hub. Delve into its past, exploring the historical ruins and lush green landscapes. Your journey then leads you to North Bay Island, a marine wonderland. Immerse yourself in the underwater marvels—snorkeling, scuba diving, or gazing at the vibrant marine life from a glass-bottom boat. As twilight paints the sky, return to Port Blair, ready for a cozy night's stay.

Day 3: Port Blair to Havelock Island

Start the day with a hearty breakfast, gearing up for your morning ferry ride to the stunning Havelock Island. Upon arrival, your resort beckons, offering a chance to unwind and soak in the island vibes. The afternoon unveils the pristine Radhanagar Beach—one of Asia's best. Here, indulge in relaxation and allow the turquoise waters to soothe your soul. The night is yours to enjoy amidst Havelock's charm.

Day 4: Kalapatthar Beach and Island Adventures

After a delicious breakfast, gear up for a day of wonders! Kalapatthar Beach is your first stop—an oasis of scenic beauty and tranquility. If adventure calls your name, choose from a range of optional activities like sea-walking or scuba diving, adding a dash of thrill to your island journey. Don't miss out on a visit to Elephant Beach for some aquatic fun. As the day winds down, let Havelock Island's magic cradle you into a restful night.

Day 5: Havelock Island to Neil Island

Enjoy a delightful breakfast and bid farewell to Havelock Island as you hop onto a ferry bound for Neil Island. Complete your check-in at the hotel and savor moments of relaxation. The white sandy shores and turquoise waters of Bharatpur Beach beckon in the afternoon, perfect for a refreshing swim or some sun-soaked lounging. As the sun dips beneath the horizon, head to Laxmanpur Beach for a stunning sunset spectacle. Unwind and drift into a peaceful night on Neil Island.

Day 6: Neil Island to Port Blair

Start your day with a scrumptious breakfast and check out from your Neil Island resort. Your morning adventure includes a visit to the Natural Coral Bridge, a captivating natural wonder. Then, it's time to set sail back to Port Blair via a morning ferry. Upon reaching, don't miss the chance to witness the captivating sunset views and the vibrant birdlife of Chidiya Tapu. Before bidding adieu, indulge in some last-minute souvenir hunting. Your Port Blair haven awaits for a night of relaxation.

    • Best Time to Visit

    Best Time to Visit

    The Andaman & Nicobar Islands are best visited during the months of October to May. Monsoon, from June to September, brings heavy rainfall, making some activities inaccessible.

    Peak Season

    December to March - The weather is ideal, with clear skies and a calm sea.Shoulder Season: October to November and April to mid-May - Less crowded, slightly hotter, but offers some discounts.

    Eco-Friendly Travel Tips

  • Avoid Single-Use Plastics:
    Bring a reusable water bottle and shopping bags.
  • Coral Care:
    Never touch or stand on corals when snorkeling or diving.
  • Support Local:
    Buy locally-made products and eat at local restaurants.
  • Reduce Waste:
    Avoid products with excessive packaging.
  • Eco-friendly Accommodations:
    Stay at hotels or resorts practicing sustainable tourism.
  • Leave No Trace:
    Ensure you clean up after your beach visits, hikes, or picnics.
